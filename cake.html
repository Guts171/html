Simple Sponge Cake

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 100g castor sugar
  • 85g sekf-raising flour

Notes on ingredients

Castor Sugar
Finely granulated white sugar.
Self-raising flour
A pre-mixed combination of flor and leavening agents (usualy salt and baking powder).

Method

1. Preheat the oven to 190Â°C.
2. Grease a 20cm round cake pan.
3. In a medium bowl, whip together the eggs and castor sugar until fluffy.
4. Fold in flour.
5. Pour mixture into the prepared pan.
6. Bake for 20 minutes in the Preheat oven, or until the top of the cake springd back when lightly pressed.
7. Cool in the pan over a wire rack.
